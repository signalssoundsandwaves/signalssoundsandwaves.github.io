About Signals Sounds And Waves

Signals, Sounds and Waves is written by me, William Eden. I recently completed a degree in Sound Engineering and Production with a 1st and felt that a blog could act as a portfolio for some of the work i have done and would be a good way of solidifying some of the concepts i have learnt. I hope this to be an informative and intuitive read.

Why Signals Sounds And Waves?

Signals, Sounds and Waves - sounds snappy right? Well there's more to it than that. There are subtle differences in these three terms; here I'll talk about what the terms mean in the context of a typical recording set up, and link to lots of useful information.
